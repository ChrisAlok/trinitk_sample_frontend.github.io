ABOUT CROP PREDICITON USING AI AND ML

Crop prediction using AI and ML refers to the application of artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques to predict the yield and quality of crops.
These techniques use historical data and real-time information such as weather patterns, soil moisture levels, and pest infestations to make predictions.
By using AI and ML, crop prediction can be made more accurate and efficient, leading to improved decision-making and resource allocation for farmers.
The use of these technologies has the potential to revolutionize agriculture and improve food security for communities around the world.
